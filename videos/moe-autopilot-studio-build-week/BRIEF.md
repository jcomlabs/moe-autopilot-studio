## Intent

Create a factual product demonstration with a memorable technical thesis: a
configuration that accelerates decode can still slow the complete workload.
Open on the decision, prove that the verdict changes when the workload changes,
then show the protocol guard, bounded three-model council, safe local runner,
and public reproducibility evidence. The product should feel like an instrument,
not a marketing dashboard.
